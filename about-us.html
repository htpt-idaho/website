Dr. Chad Simmons

Chad Simmons was born in Idaho Falls, ID and grew up in Iona, ID., where his love for small towns developed. Chad had several experiences in life that cultivated his passion for Physical therapy. A big one was his Dad being paralyzed in an accident when Chad was a young child, as well as, his many sport injuries he obtained while in high school. He saw the difference therapy could make in a person's quality of life and he desired to help people live better.

Chad is a graduate of Brigham Young University - Idaho and attended Bellarmine Physical Therapy in Kentucky to receive his Doctor of Physical Therapy.

Chad loves being a Dad and enjoys spending time with his wife and three little boys. He also enjoys playing sports, boating, and spending time outdoors.

We are excited to be your Hometown Physical Therapist and look forward to meeting you.
